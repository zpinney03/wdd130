About Us

About us - it all started when we heard about famous donuts made from potatoes, called “spudnuts”. We knew we had to get in on the fun. We spent many hours, perfecting our own recipes of delectable donuts, so that they are the best of the best. We realized we couldn’t enjoy these just for ourselves. We want to share our take on the “Spudnut” with you. We “donut disappoint”.

Community Involvement - We love to be involved with the community.
