Menu

Menu - Choose from our classic “spudnut” donuts. We offer maple, chocolate, and strawberry bars. Our very own warm donuts: Blueberry roll, cinnamon roll, and waffle spudnuts. Donuts based on cookies: “Oreo”, “Nutter Butter”, and “Chips Ahoy”. We even serve milk, chocolate milk, and hot chocolate. You can click on the button below to see the prices on our menu. - Monthly specials: Be on the lookout for our monthly specials. We always love trying something new. If you have any suggestions, we would love to hear them. You can message us at donuthutllc@gmail.com. - Rewards program - We love our customers, and we want to reward you for your loyalty. Join our rewards program today! - Online Order - We work to make you happy. We know you have busy lives. Click on this link, where you will be able to place an order. Whether it’s for your family gathering, work event, or party, you may place an order by clicking the button below. We have many choices for you to choose from. Let us be a part of your big event.

Images for the menu page

About Us

About us - it all started when we heard about famous donuts made from potatoes, called “spudnuts”. We knew we had to get in on the fun. We spent many hours, perfecting our own recipes of delectable donuts, so that they are the best of the best. We realized we couldn’t enjoy these just for ourselves. We want to share our take on the “Spudnut” with you. We “donut disappoint”. - Community Involvement - We love to be involved with the community.
